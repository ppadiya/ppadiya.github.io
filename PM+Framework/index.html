Product Launch Framework

This framework outlines the key phases, activities, and responsibilities for launching the product in the specified region. Click on each phase to expand its details.

Goal

Understand the market, define the strategy, and identify all requirements.

Key Activities

  • Market & Competitive Analysis
  • Legal & Compliance Deep Dive (incl. GDPR/Local Law, PCI DSS)
  • Localization Scope Definition
  • Initial Pricing & Billing Research
  • Data Security & Infrastructure Assessment
  • Resource Planning

Checklist & Responsibilities

Task Responsible Party Status
Market Analysis Report Complete Product Marketing, Market Research ☐ Pending
Comprehensive Legal/Compliance Report Legal, Compliance, Security ☐ Pending
GDPR/Local Privacy Law Gap Analysis Legal, Compliance, Security ☐ Pending
PCI DSS Regional Applicability Confirmed Compliance, Security, Finance ☐ Pending
Localization Requirements Documented Product Management, Localization Team ☐ Pending
Preliminary Pricing & Billing Analysis Product Marketing, Finance ☐ Pending
Data Security/Infrastructure Needs Assessed Security, Engineering, IT Operations ☐ Pending
Initial Resource Plan & Budget Approved Project Management, Leadership ☐ Pending

Goal

Adapt the product, systems, and processes to meet regional requirements.

Key Activities

  • Product Localization (UI/UX, Language)
  • Legal & Compliance Implementation (Consent, Policies, Controls)
  • Billing Process Design & Implementation (Currency, Tax, Payment Methods, PCI DSS)
  • Pricing Strategy Formulation & Setup
  • Data Security Protocol Implementation (Encryption, Access Control, Breach Plan)
  • Documentation Creation (Drafts)

Checklist & Responsibilities

Task Responsible Party Status

Compliance Features Implemented (Dev/Test Env) Engineering, Legal, Compliance ☐ Pending
Localized Legal Documents Drafted Legal ☐ Pending
Billing System Configured & Tested (Internal) Finance, Engineering, Compliance ☐ Pending
Payment Gateway Integration & PCI Test Engineering, Security, Finance ☐ Pending
Final Pricing Strategy Approved & Implemented Product Marketing, Finance, Sales ☐ Pending
Data Security Controls Verified Security, Engineering ☐ Pending
Data Residency Compliance Confirmed Legal, Compliance, Security, Engineering ☐ Pending
Draft Documentation Set Created Tech Writing, Product Mktg, Finance, Legal ☐ Pending

Goal

Prepare internal teams, finalize assets, and conduct final testing.

Key Activities

  • Sales Readiness Initiatives (Training, Collateral)
  • Marketing & Communications Plan Finalization
  • Support Readiness (Training, Knowledge Base)
  • Delivery & Implementation Alignment
  • End-to-End Testing (Staging/Beta)
  • Final Legal & Compliance Review/Sign-off
  • Finalize Documentation

Checklist & Responsibilities

Task Responsible Party Status
Sales Team Trained & Certified Sales Enablement, Sales Leadership ☐ Pending
Localized Sales Collateral Ready Product Marketing, Sales ☐ Pending
Marketing Launch Plan & Assets Finalized Marketing, PR ☐ Pending

Delivery/Onboarding Process Confirmed Operations, Customer Success, Eng. ☐ Pending
End-to-End System Testing Complete QA, Engineering, Finance, Product Mgmt ☐ Pending
Beta Program Feedback Incorporated (if any) Product Management, QA ☐ Pending
Final Legal/Compliance Sign-off Received Legal, Compliance ☐ Pending
All Documentation Finalized & Translated Tech Writing, Localization ☐ Pending

Goal

Successfully introduce the product to the market.

Key Activities

  • Execute Marketing & PR Plan
  • Enable Sales Channels
  • Activate Systems (Production, Billing)
  • Monitor Systems (Performance, Security, Transactions)
  • Provide Launch Support

Goal

Track performance, gather feedback, ensure ongoing compliance, and iterate.

Key Activities

  • Performance Tracking (Regional KPIs)
  • Feedback Collection (Customers, Sales, Support)
  • Ongoing Compliance Reviews & Audits
  • Optimization & Iteration (Product, Pricing, Process)
  • Stakeholder Reporting
